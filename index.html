Yifu Zhang

I obtained my Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of California, San Diego in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Jorge Cortés. Before that, I received my B.Eng. in Automation from the Harbin Institute of Technology in 2014.

Currently I'm a Senior Software Engineer at Bloomberg.
